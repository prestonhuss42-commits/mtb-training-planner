# MTB Training Planner

A user-friendly web app that helps mountain bikers plan their training schedules based on their discipline (XC, Enduro, Downhill), goals (fitness or race-focused), season, available training days, and preferred mileage. It generates personalized ride and workout recommendations for trail and road/trainer sessions to optimize your training and enjoyment.

---

## Features

- Choose your discipline: Cross-Country (XC), Enduro, or Downhill  
- Select your training goal: Stay Fit / Have Fun or Race Focused  
- Pick your training phase/season: Off-season, Pre-season, Race season, Post-season  
- Specify how many days per week you want to train  
- Select which days you can ride trails and which days must be road/trainer rides  
- Input your preferred miles per day and get feedback on your training volume  
- Generates a customized weekly training plan with daily recommendations  

---

## How to Use

1. Open the `index.html` file in a modern web browser  
2. Use the dropdown menus and inputs to set your preferences  
3. Click the **Generate Schedule** button  
4. View your personalized weekly training plan and mileage feedback
